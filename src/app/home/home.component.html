Langcab is a simple webapp designed for capturing your vocabulary.

When learning a language, it's not always easy to remember all the vocabulary. Langcab will help you remember your words by using a spaced repetition algorithm.
Even the process of adding words into the system will steady your vocabulary. You can add as many languages as you want.
To use it, just sign in and start by adding your first word!

Why use Langcab

Spaced repetition

Langcab will automatically detect how fast you learn and lets you recall every word often enough so you don't forget it but just as often as necessary to remember. This is currently the most effective way known, to study vocabulary.

Bring your vocabulary everywhere

Langcab saves your words online and allows you to access your words anywhere you have an internet connection. No matter if you're on your phone, browser, or desktop, as long as you log in, you can improve your vocabulary.

Find your words

While learning a language, it is common to forget a word learned in class. When translating it, many results appear on the screen, and you don't know which one applies. With Langcab, you can create your own dictionary and find the words you've learned immediately.

From learners for learners

Developed by people who are passionate about learning languages, any feedback is more than welcome. If you have any suggestions on how to improve this website, please go ahead and send us a message via email: langcab@web.de.

Multiple languages

If you are learning multiple languages at a time, Langcab gives you the option of adding as many languages as you want. You are also not limited to the original version of a word. You could learn from Spanish to English or from German to Chinese. Up to you!

The best way to use Langcab

Learn by writing

Many studies have that writing a word helps us remember it especially for languages with a different alphabet. We recommend physically writing down new vocabulary while trainning your ability and adding new terms in Langcab. Using spaced repetition, algorithms, and physically writing words down, studying will be much more effective. To avoid wasting paper, it may be useful to buy a small white board.

Constantly add words

Try to set a goal for an amount of words you want to learn per week. It's important to constantly add words to your dictionary to avoid plateaus. If you constantly increase your active vocabulary, you can make sure that you are still improving. The app will take care of making sure you remember the words entered in the past.
