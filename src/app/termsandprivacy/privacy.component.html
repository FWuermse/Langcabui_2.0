Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how langcab.com ("langcab.com", "We", or "Us") collects and uses personal information that you ("you" or "your") provide on the langcab.com website and on the langcab.com App (collectively "langcab.com"). This privacy policy was last updated on July 19th, 2019.

Guiding Principles

Our privacy policy is guided by the following principles:

  1. We’re committed to creating a safe and secure online environment for you.
  2. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  3. We collect very little personally identifiable information about you, and what we do collect we store securely.
  4. We believe that ultimately, you own your data, and that, whenever possible, you get to decide whether to take it with you.
  5. We believe that improving your vocabulary is a worthwhile lifelong pursuit. Therefore, unless you tell us otherwise, we will keep your learning history so that we can continue to personalize your experience over time.

How we collect data

langcab.com collects the following information about you and your use of langcab.com:

Personal Information

When you register for langcab.com, you provide to us personal information (such as your full name, email address, grade level and school affiliation) that could be used to identify you. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, we call this “Personal Information”. Your Nickname is used to identify you on to other users on langcab.com. If you do not want to be identified, do not use any part of your real name as your nickname.

Information from Third Party Authentication Providers like Facebook, Github, Twitter or Google

If you decide to register through or otherwise grant access to a third-party authentication provider (“Authentication Provider”), such as Facebook Connect or Google, langcab.com may also collect Personal Information that is already associated with your Authentication Provider account. You may also have the option of sharing additional information with langcab.com through an Authentication Provider. If you choose to provide such information, during registration or otherwise, you are giving langcab.com the permission to use, share, and store it in a manner consistent with this Privacy Policy.

Information about Your Use of langcab.com

We collect usage information about certain features of langcab.com, such as the questions you’ve answered, the words mastered, the effort it takes you to remember a specific word and the amount of time you’ve spent on langcab.com.

Information you Provide as Part of Your User Profile

Users have a public profile page on langcab.com. This page presents information that you provide such as your nickname. The profile page may also show information about your use of langcab.com such as achievements earned, lists created, or your level, as well as any comments that you’ve made on the langcab.com.

If your personal data changes or you want to remove your account please contact us via email shown in the Contact form.

Information You Provide while Using langcab.com

Users of langcab.com can create various types of content ("User Content") that may be displayed publicly on langcab.com. User Content includes information such as vocabulary lists, comments, and other types of material that is shared with the community.

Any User Content, along with your nickname or username, become public and may be used by us. Your public activities may also be read and collected by other users of the public activity areas on langcab.com. We are not responsible for the personally identifiable information you provide in these forums. The vocabulary you store belongs and can be used by langcab.com at any time even if it's referenced as "Your words", "Your vocabulary", "Your data", "Your vocab" or similar. We are not liable for the vocabulary you enter on langcab.com and do not provide support for missing words or any other kind date you entered.

What we will NOT do with your Personal Information

langcab.com will NOT sell Personal Information provided by you or by your School or District (except in connection of a merger or acquisition).

langcab.com will NOT use Personal Information provided by you:

  • To engage in targeted advertising
  • To build a profile about you.

How we use Data

langcab.com uses collected Personal Information in the following ways:

To Enhance Your Experience on langcab.com

langcab.com uses the information you provide or that we collect to enhance our relationship with you and to operate, maintain, enhance, and provide all of the features and services found on langcab.com. langcab.com, for instance, stores your responses to the questions you’ve answered and the words you’ve had trouble with so we can provide you with the right question at the right time to improve your vocabulary. We may use your Personal Information to provide you with information about langcab.com’s features, services and other offerings that may be of interest to you. Under certain circumstances and if we obtain your consent (for example, if you submit a testimonial and agree to make it public), we may post your Personal Information on langcab.com.

To Understand How You and Others Use langcab.com

langcab.com uses all of the information that you provide or that we collect from other users to understand and analyze the usage trends, learning behaviors, and preferences of our users, to improve the way langcab.com work and look, and to create new features and functionality. For more information, please see the “Technologies” section, below.

Technical Information

To provide a personalized learning and high-quality experience, we may use various technologies that automatically record certain technical information from your browser or device, including standard log files, web beacons, or pixel tags. This technical information may include your Internet Protocol (IP) address, device or browser type, Internet service provider (ISP), referring or exit pages, clickstream data, operating system, and the dates and times that you visit langcab.com. We do this to better understand how you are using langcab.com so we can improve functionality and the services we offer you.

Like most websites, whether or not you are a registered member, we may send one or more cookies – small text files containing a string of alphanumeric characters – to your computer. Cookies remember information about your activities on a website and enable us to provide you with a more personalized learning experience. langcab.com may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. A session cookie disappears automatically after you close your browser. A persistent cookie remains after you close your browser and may be used by your browser on subsequent visits to langcab.com. You can, however, remove a persistent cookie at any time. Please review your web browser “Help” file to learn the proper way to modify your cookie settings. However, without cookies you will not have access to certain services and features on langcab.com.

langcab.com uses a variety of third-party service providers, such as Google Analytics to understand usage. We may allow third-party service providers to place and read their own cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information through langcab.com. This information is collected directly and automatically by these third parties, and langcab.com does not participate in these data transmissions. langcab.com will, wherever feasible, contractually obligate our third-party service providers to abide by the terms of this Privacy Notice.

langcab.com also uses Local Storage Objects (LSOs) such as HTML5 to store content information and preferences. Third parties with whom we partner to provide certain features on langcab.com may use LSOs such as HTML5 to collect and store information. Various browsers may offer their own management tools for removing HTML5 LSOs. Please consult your browser’s “Help” function to learn more.
